Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how virtcoin collects, uses, and protects information from users who visit our website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (such as by joining a waitlist or contacting us).
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifying technical information such as browser type, device type, and basic site analytics to improve our service.
  • Wallet Address: If you connect your crypto wallet, we may temporarily access your public wallet address to enable site features.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve our website and features.
  • To respond to your inquiries or requests.
  • To communicate important updates about Virtcoin (if you opt-in).

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to provide requested services.

Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no website can guarantee 100% security.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (like Discord or Twitter). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Your Choices

  • You can contact us at ethan@virtcoin.com to request removal of your information from our records.
  • You may disconnect your wallet at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at ethan@virtcoin.com.
